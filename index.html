About Us

Straight to the Point Archery is a non-profit education center founded in 2005 by Giap Nguyen. Our state-of-the-art educational and training facility incorporates detailed technical video analysis combined with generations of archery experience from certified coaches and instructors.

Giap Nguyen started Straight to the Point Archery in 2005. He's an avid and accomplished archer himself, having competed with the United States Archery Team in the 2000 Summer Olympics. He has three children, and started Straight to the Point Archery in part because he was not satisfied with the archery education options for them in Tacoma.
